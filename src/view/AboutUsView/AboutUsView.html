Cooperation

Throughout the project, we actively cooperated with each other: we communicated in discord chats, held meetings every weekday, reported on the work done. At the end of each sprint, a retrospective was held, opinions were exchanged, and ideas for improving communications were offered. They adequately perceived each other's criticism, tried to distribute tasks equally.

We were supported by a mentor, also participated in rallies. In the second sprint, there were conflicts regarding code review and priority of tasks, but we managed to solve these problems and improve communication within the team.

Thanks to our joint work, the project turned out to be successful and each of us gained experience in team development and implementation of a real project.
